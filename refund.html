Refund Policy for Platinum Jubilee Registrations

Thank you for registering for the Platinum Jubilee event. Please carefully read the following refund policy before completing your registration.

No Refund Policy After Registration

Once the registration is completed, we do not offer any refunds. This includes the registration fee and any other associated costs. By registering, you acknowledge and agree that all payments are final.

Event Cancellation by Committee

In the event that the Platinum Jubilee is canceled by the organizing committee, a full refund of the registration fee will be provided to all registered participants. The refund process will be initiated within 7 business days of the cancellation announcement.

Non-Transferable Registration

Registrations are non-transferable. The registration fee cannot be transferred to another individual or event.

Event Postponement

If the event is postponed, registrations will automatically be transferred to the new date. Refunds will not be issued for postponements unless explicitly stated by the organizing committee.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this refund policy, please contact our support team at +918207045511.

By completing the registration, you agree to the terms of this refund policy.
