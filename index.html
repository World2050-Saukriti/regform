Who We Are

Welcome to Mahatma Srijunga Govt. Sr. Sec. School, Bermiok Martam, West Sikkim. For 75 years, we have been dedicated to shaping young minds and fostering a culture of academic excellence.

Why We Are Celebrating

Our Platinum Jubilee is a time to reflect on our achievements, honor our legacy, and celebrate the countless students and staff who have contributed to our success. Join us in this joyous occasion!
