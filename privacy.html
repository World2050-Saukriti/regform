Kaun Banega Lakhpati Contest Privacy Policy

Introduction:

This Privacy Policy explains how the organizer of the Kaun Banega Lakhpati contest (the "Quiz Contest") collects, uses, and protects personal information and payment data from participants. By registering for the Contest, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection:

To participate in the Contest, you will be required to provide the following information:

1. Personal Details:

  • - Name
  • - Address
  • - Phone Number
  • - Email Address
  • - Age

2. Payment Information (for entry fee/payment):

  • - Card Type (Credit/Debit)
  • - Card Number
  • - Expiration Date
  • - CVV
  • - Payment transaction ID

3. Contest-related Information:

  • - Answers to contest questions
  • - Score and ranking

Payment Gateway:

Our registration website integrates a secure payment gateway to facilitate entry fee/payment. The payment gateway uses industry-standard encryption (SSL) to protect your payment information.

Use of Information:

We use collected information for:

  • 1. Contest administration and management
  • 2. Communication with participants (results, updates, and notifications)
  • 3. Verification of participant identity and eligibility
  • 4. Compliance with applicable laws and regulations
  • 5. Analyzing Contest performance and improving future contests

Data Protection and Security:

We implement reasonable physical, technical, and administrative measures to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our payment gateway adheres to industry standards for secure payment processing.

Data Sharing and Disclosure:

We may share your information with:

  • 1. Contest sponsors and partners (with your consent)
  • 2. Government authorities (as required by law)
  • 3. Our service providers (for Contest administration and technical support)
  • 4. Auditors and compliance regulators

Retention and Deletion:

We retain your information for the duration of the Contest and as required by law. After the Contest, we will delete or anonymize your information, except for winners' information, which will be retained for record-keeping purposes.

Your Rights:

  • 1. Access and correction of your information
  • 2. Withdrawal from the Contest (prior to submission)
  • 3. Opt-out from future communications

Governing Law and Jurisdiction:

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Sikkim State. Any disputes arising from this Policy will be resolved through the Dispute Resolution Mechanism.

Changes to this Policy:

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.
